What is Parallax?

Parallax scrolling is a web design technique in which the website background moves at a slower pace than the foreground. This results in a 3D effect as you scroll down the page, adding a sense of depth and creating a more immersive browsing experience.

This technique can be used to create a variety of effects, from subtle animations to full-screen interactive experiences. It is a popular choice for single-page websites, portfolios, and product landing pages.
